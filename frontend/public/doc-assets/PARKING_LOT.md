## Deferred: Commerce and Inventory

These ideas are valuable but not active scope yet:

- product catalog
- barcode and QR code generation
- inventory lots
- stock movements
- online orders
- shipments
- delivery status
- customers
- POS integration
- batch/lot traceability

## Rule for AI Coding Agents

Do not implement commerce, inventory, barcode, QR, POS, order, shipment, or customer-management features unless a later milestone explicitly activates them.
